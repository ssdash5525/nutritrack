How it works

BMR — Basal Metabolic Rate Calories your body burns just to stay alive (sleeping, breathing). Calculated with the Mifflin-St Jeor equation using your weight, height, age, and sex.
TDEE — Total Daily Energy Expenditure BMR × your activity multiplier. This is how many calories you burn in a typical day including exercise.
Calorie Target TDEE adjusted for your goal: −500 kcal to lose ~0.5 kg/week, +300 kcal to slowly gain. This is automatically applied across the app when you save.
